Campus Life

RAI grant centre

Was established on the 4th of may 2020

THe RAI Grant centre is a physical education area which includes an olympic-sized swimming pool, three basketball courts, a gymnasium, a gym, PDHPE classrooms and Christian Studies classrooms. This building is name after R A I Grant, the headmaster of this school from 1984-2002.



BH travers centre

established in 2000 and renovated in 2020

The BH Travers Centre includes the Playfair Hall, Boer War Contingent Memorial Library, the Information Technology Department. This building was named after by a former headmaster B H Travers, headmaster from (1959-1984)



Holtermann Tower

established in 1925

The Holtermann Tower, includes School Boarding House and The Headmasters Office, this building was home of the famous gold miner Otto Holtermann and then the home of two famous photograhers but then our school removed it and replicated it to make School House, now on the same area is The Graythwaite building
